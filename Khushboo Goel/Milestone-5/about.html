Our History

A trip down our memory lane

Our passion for crafting the perfect flavors and creamiest textures using only the finest ingredients is what has set the Gelato brand apart. We’ve never wavered on the principles that made us who we are from the very beginning. Today, the Gelato brand continues to transform the finest ingredients into extraordinary experiences bite by bite.

Our range is produced under stringent quality norms using high quality milk, cream, chocolate, fruits and other natural ingredients to assure high nutritional value and low fat content. Our cold storage unit stores each variety of ice cream under requisite temperature so as to avoid re-crystallization and maintain taste and freshness. We carefully and hygienically pack our ice creams in tamper proof material so as to maintain their quality, taste and aroma.

Gelato is committed to using only the best ingredients the world has to offer. So we knew we had to act when the bees that pollinate one-third of the foods we eat, including many of the ingredients we use every day, started to struggle. Despite all the success, the philosophy behind the business remains simple, the same as that of the founder of the house : ‘A middle-class neighbourhood ice cream destination that is synonymous with quality and variety of flavours at affordable prices’
