Question-1

Every country measures temperature according to different scales. Being a scientist at the Temperature department it is your job to provide the reading according to all scales. Write a program to convert a given temperature reading (in Kelvin) to celsius and Fahrenheit.


<script>
var tempC, tempK=273.15, tempF;
tempK=prompt("Enter temperature in Kelvin : ");
tempC=tempK-273.15;
tempF=((tempK - 273.15)*9)/5 + 32;
alert(`Temprature in Fahrenheit : ${tempF}`);
alert("Temprature in Celsius : "+tempC);
</script>

Question-2

Your puppy dog grew much faster compared to your small brother. Confused, you went to the pet shop and the owner told that dogs age differently than humans. Write a program to convert given Human years into Dog years. (1 human year is equal to 8 dog years)

<script>
var human, dog;
human=prompt("Enter Human years : ",human);
dog=human*8;
alert(human + " Human 👩👨 years are equivalent to " + dog + " Dog 🐶 years !! ");
</script>
