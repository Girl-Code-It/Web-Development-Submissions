Fandumb

Shafaqt Amanat Ali Khan

I'm not going to bore you with wikipedia details, but just to give you a brief background, he is a 55 yr old Pakistani singer. He has a band named 'Fuzön' (literal gem).

Guess who sung 'Mitwa'? Yep, that's right! It's Shafaqt Amanat Ali. Some of his renowned works include 'Tu he mera' from 'Jannat 2' , 'Manchala' from 'Hasee Toh Phasee' ,'Darmiyaan' from 'Jodi Breakers' and the list goes on.. He has literally blessed the bollywood music industry with his soulful voice.

Remember the band I mentioned 'Fuzön'? 'Khamaaj' to this date remains the most popular song by the band. You just can't have enough of his music. I know that by now I must be sounding like a total fangirl but, that's what this page is about, right?
