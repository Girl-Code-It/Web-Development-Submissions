ONLINE REGISTERATION FORM

Instructions & Information for Online Registration

  1. Submit the details online and pay Registration Fee of Rs. 25/- online. You will receive an acknowledgement mail containing registration number and online payment details.
  2. After submission the concerned person will get back to you and fix online interaction session to take your registration forward.
  3. Incomplete form will be rejected.

Self attested documents to be uploaded along with the Registration Form:

  1. Child's-Birth Certificate, Aadhar card, and 2 Photographs.
  2. Parent's/Guardian's-Aadhar Card and 2 Photographs each. Self attested documents required at the time of admission.
  3. Child's-Aadhar Card/4 Photographs and TC original (K.G.Onwards)
  4. Parent's/Guardian's-Aadhar Card/Address Proof and 4 Photographs each.
